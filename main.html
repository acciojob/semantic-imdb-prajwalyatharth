Plot Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully plant an idea in a person's mind, known as inception. Alongside his team, Cobb faces the challenges of dream layers and the emotional toll of his past.
